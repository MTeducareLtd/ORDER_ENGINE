Lead Definition

  • A Contact represents a person conducting business with your company.
  • Contact could be acting individually or representing an Organization.
  • Contacts are of two different categories: Primary and Secondary
  • Primary contacts are the potential customers to whom the product or service is being sold and billed to.
  • Secondary contacts are used for marketing and sales campaigns only and also could be decision makers for the deal
  • A contact can be created independently and then assigned to a lead either through campaign or directly from contacts
